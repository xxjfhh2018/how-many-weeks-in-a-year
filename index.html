How many Weeks are in a Year

One Gregorian calendar year has approximately 52 weeks

Common year: 52.14 weeks per common year

Leap year: 52.28 weeks per leap year

Copy and Share Share on Facebook

How to calculate weeks in a year

To calculate the number of weeks in a year, simply divide the total number of days in a year by the number of days in a week. A typical year has 365 days, and a week has 7 days. Dividing 365 days by 7 gives you approximately 52.14 weeks.

How to calculate weeks in a year

Weeks in a common Year

Common year: 365 days per common year / 7 days per week = 52.1429 weeks per common year

Weeks in a leap year

Leap year: 366 days per leap year / 7 days per week = 52.2857 weeks per leap year
